# AeroCafé — Brand Context & Guardrails

## Identity
AeroCafé is a high-altitude specialty coffee bar for airport travelers who
refuse to drink bad coffee between flights. Calm, premium, fast.

## Voice
- Confident, warm, never bro-y. Short sentences.
- Talk about the coffee and the calm, not "hustle".
- No exclamation-point spam. One, maybe, ever.

## Visual constraints (hard guardrails)
- Palette: deep espresso brown (#2b1d14), warm cream (#f4ece2), one accent
  (burnt amber, around #d98e3b). No neon, no gradients-on-gradients.
- Typography: one serif for headlines, one sans for body. Generous spacing.
- Layout: single-column, mobile-first, max content width ~720px.
- Tone of imagery: warm, quiet, lots of negative space.

## Must include
- A headline and a sub-headline.
- 3 short feature/section blocks.
- One clear call-to-action (e.g. "Find AeroCafé in your terminal").

## Must NOT include
- Stock-photo clichés in copy ("game-changer", "synergy", "elevate your day").
- More than one accent color.
- Auto-playing anything.
